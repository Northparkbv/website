Inside the CASIO Classwiz calculators

My calculator is the fx-85GT X.

 

Have you ever wondered if there is more to these calculators? Well here’s the answer.

 

DEV MENUS

Hold down SHIFT, AC and MENU when powering on the calculator. The calculator should display:

You then need to press one of these numbers quickly:

8

This tests a select amount of keys, checks the solar panel and a few other things.

9

Cycles through most test options. Press SHIFT to go to the next one. Order: Produces a very big number, and then does a screen test. Says some random words that I don’t understand, then does a long key test. Then goes to the advanced contrast select screen. After this, it will let you reset the calculator by pressing AC, but you can press ON to ignore this.

6

DIAGNOSTIC

 

 

Press AC

 
 
Most user friendly. Shows a menu:

Display: tests the pixels of your display.

Version&SUM: Displays your system info.

Key test: A long key test.

Note for the long key test: The first few keys are SHIFT, ALPHA, LEFT, RIGHT, MENU, OPTN, X­­­­3. DOWN, RIGHT and then from then on it’s simple.
